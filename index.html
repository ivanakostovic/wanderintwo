Explore a new city

Check out these different city-break destinations for some inspiration!

We don't know about you but we are always excited to explore a new city. Here are some that we've fallen in love with during our travels this year.

Istanbul

The 'City on Seven Hills' spans both Europe and Asia and is a melting-pot of culture with amazing history, mouth-watering food and lots of cats

Rome

Brimming with ancient history and architecture the 'Eternal City' is one of those classic must-see places that is always buzzing

Edinburgh

'Auld Reekie' is full of cobbled streets, cosy pubs, shortcuts, lovely views, friendly people and charm that will steal your heart

Madrid

Indulge in art, nightlife, tapas and busy open-air public squares and gardens in the city of 'The Bear and the Strawberry Tree'
