Has Coronavirus arrived in Bali?

It’s hard to tell as we are currently being told by the World Health Organization and other nations that Indonesia is underreporting the number of those diagnosed and fatalities.

Right now the outside world feels like a movie as everyone goes about as if it’s business as usual here in Canggu.

As the threat of borders closing and flights being cancelled looms, I have a dilemma whether to stay in Bali and hope for the best or fly to Perth, Australia to be reunited with my Fiance.

However, if I choose to fly back to Perth there is a catch…
