What we offer

Collaborations

It's our pleasure to work with travel-related experiences, brands, services and products. There are many ways we can collaboate and deliver content across our blog and social media or even create assets you can use on your channels. If you have an idea or a proposal let us know via email!

Sposored Posts

We love showcasing travel-related services, products and experiences that can provide value to our audience's travels and adventures - whether it's via our blog or social media. Get in touch with us and we'll be happy to have a conversation and also share our rates and terms and conditions with you.

Press Trips

We are available for solo or group press trips and media visits to promote any travel-related experiences, locations, brands or products. We are happy working to itineraries, briefs and recommendations or can research and explore ourselves!

Photography

Our Mavic Pro drone and Sony a7iii plus all our snazzy accessories are the perfect tools to create beautiful imagery. We can help with all sorts of image-based content such as for products, services, food and beverage, real estate, tours, accommodation, and much more - just ask!

Guest Posts

We are always on the lookout for talented guest-bloggers to to bring valuable content to our Wander In Two audience. If you have a unique travel-related experience or tips about life on the road, social media or photography check out our guest blogger page for more information.

Podcast

Jamie's Wee Podcast discusses a whole host of different topics and subjects. Do you have a product or service which you would like to sponsor on the podcast? For more information or to discuss further send Jamie an email at hello@wanderintwo.com
